AI Feedback Process

Automated extraction, classification, and routing of university feedback — replacing manual officer reading with AI-powered batch processing and structured email delivery to school deans and service heads.

🗺️ Product Roadmap
Three stages from a testable AI hypothesis to a trusted institutional tool
MTP — Minimum Testable Product
Does the AI extract correctly?
Validate that AI can reliably identify school/service mentions, classify sentiment, and produce accurate CSVs — before building any UI or email plumbing.
  • Ingest batch of JSON feedback files
  • Extract relevant text per school/service
  • Classify each mention as positive or negative
  • Output: 2 CSVs per school/service (pos/neg mentions + feedback ID)
  • Officer reviews output in a basic UI
→
MUP — Minimum Usable Product
Can officers run this in their workflow?
Make the tool usable end-to-end: officer triggers batch, reviews AI output, approves, and system sends emails with CSV attachments to deans — with a full audit trail.
  • Web dashboard for batch management
  • Review and approve/reject routing per school/service
  • Automated email dispatch with CSVs attached
  • Audit log of all sent batches
  • Manual batch trigger
→
MVP — Minimum Viable Product
Can leadership trust and use this data?
Add historical storage and an analytics layer so officers, deans, and leadership can track sentiment trends, identify systemic issues, and reference past feedback batches.
  • Historical feedback database
  • Sentiment trend chart (per school/service over time)
  • Most complained services/schools ranking
  • Role-based access: officer, dean, leadership
  • Report export (CSV / PDF)
👥 User Personas
Three distinct users with fundamentally different needs from the same system
📋
Ms. Nguyen Thi Thu
Quality Assurance Officer
Goal: Process a full feedback batch in one working session, not over several days.
Pain: Manually reading 200 feedbacks per cycle, extracting mentions, and formatting CSVs takes 3–4 days and is error-prone.
Context: Works in batches at semester milestones. Needs to verify AI accuracy before trusting it to send emails.
"I just need to check that the AI got it right and hit send. I don't want to read every feedback myself."
MTP MUP MVP
🎓
Dr. Nguyen Minh Tuan
School Dean (IT School)
Goal: Receive organized, school-specific feedback so he can prepare concrete responses and commitments for students.
Pain: Currently receives broad feedback summaries or nothing at all — no specifics to act on, no way to identify recurring issues.
Context: Reads feedback after semester ends. Needs positive and negative separated so he can respond specifically to each.
"I need to know what specifically my school is being criticized or praised for — not a general summary."
MUP MVP
🏛️
Prof. Le Van Duc
University Leadership (Vice-Rector)
Goal: See institutional sentiment trends to guide strategic planning and resource allocation decisions.
Pain: Currently has no systematic view across feedback types and schools — decisions are based on anecdote and spot checks.
Context: Reviews analytics quarterly. Needs cross-school, cross-service comparisons over time — not individual feedback content.
"Which services are consistently generating complaints? Show me the trend, not just this semester."

Stage As a… I want to… So that… Acceptance criteria
MTP Officer Upload a folder of feedback JSON files and trigger AI extraction I don't have to process each file manually All JSONs parsed; extraction runs without manual file-by-file handling
MTP Officer See which feedback sections the AI attributed to each school and service I can verify the AI isn't routing things incorrectly Each attribution shows feedback ID + quoted section + school/service name
MTP Officer See positive and negative mentions separated for each school/service The CSV output matches what deans need to respond to students Two CSVs per recipient: one positive, one negative, each with feedback ID column
MTP Officer Mark a batch review as "checked" after spot-checking the AI output I have a record that a human verified this batch before it was acted on Batch moves from "pending review" to "reviewed" state; timestamp logged
MUP Officer Approve or reject routing to specific schools/services before any email is sent I maintain control over what reaches recipients, even if most routing is correct Each school/service routing can be individually approved or skipped; no email sends without approval
MUP Officer Trigger email dispatch to all approved recipients in one action I don't have to compose emails manually for each school and service Each approved recipient gets one email with two CSV attachments; sending confirmed with delivery log
MUP Dean Receive a structured email with my school's positive and negative feedback CSVs I can prepare specific responses and commitments for students Email contains batch date, feedback type, and two attached CSVs; feedback IDs link to source files
MUP Officer View an audit log of all batches that were sent, to whom, and when I can answer questions about what was sent and reference prior batches Log shows batch ID, date, recipients, # of feedbacks, and sent/skipped status per recipient
MVP Officer Search historical feedback by school, service, date range, or feedback type I can reference past feedback when questions arise from schools or leadership Search returns matching feedbacks with metadata; clicking a result shows the full extracted content
MVP Leadership View a sentiment trend chart per school and service over time I can identify whether issues are improving or worsening across semesters Chart filterable by school, service, feedback type, and date range; shows pos/neg ratio per period
MVP Leadership See a ranked list of the most complained-about schools and services I can prioritize where to direct improvement resources Ranking shows complaint volume + sentiment score; filterable by feedback type and period
MVP Dean Log in and view analytics filtered to my school only I can track my school's feedback trends without needing to contact the officer Dean login shows only their school's data; no cross-school data visible
MVP Officer / Leadership Export analytics data as CSV or PDF I can include it in institutional reports and committee presentations Export covers the currently filtered view; PDF includes charts, CSV includes raw numbers
